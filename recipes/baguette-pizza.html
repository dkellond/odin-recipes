Baguette Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 french baguette
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2/3 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 2 ounces cooked and crumbled sausage
  • 2 ounces mini pepperoni slices

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees

  2. Step 2: Split baguette in half lengthwise. Warm pizza sauce in a microwave-safe bowl until hot, about 45 seconds.

  3. Step 3: Spread pizza sauce on baguette, sprinkle on cheese, and scatter sausage and pepperoni on top as desired. Place pizza onto a baking tray.

  4. Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is golden, about 18 minutes. Optional step: turn on the ovenâ€™s broiler, set a rack 6 inches below the heating element, and broil the pizza for a deeper color, 1 to 2 minutes.

  5. Step 5: Cut pizza crosswise into slices. Serve and enjoy.
